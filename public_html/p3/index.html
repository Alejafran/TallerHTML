Ice cream shop

It is a pleasure to welcome you to the exciting world of ice cream. Our ice cream shop is a profitable business concept that was born in 2014 with great strength and success. In a nutshell, Ice Cream Sweet Cream is an attractive family style ice cream franchise model that offers for everyone: A unique place with exquisite ice cream at the best price in town. As we seek to expand our presence to other cities in Colombia we hope to forge the symbol of quality for clients like you.We hope to join your desire and enthusiasm with our professionalism, quality, consistency and a true passion for excellence.
